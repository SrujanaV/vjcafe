About us

Vaibhav Jain, originally from jewery and construction industry, founded VJ cafe in Vile parle, mumbai in 2015 after he realised his love for food is unstoppable.. He made the decsision to start a fast food cafe serving 20+ varieties of fries, twisted potato, milkshakes, wraps and burgers. After 2 years of amazing response from students and across social media, he decided to start franchising.

VJ Cafe is a prce sensitive brand as it attracts lot of young college crowd across the cities. We are coming up with multiple more variations like Bottled milkshakes, gravy fries etc.

USP - The unique flavors of fries and the most selling, twisted potato. It’s an open kitchen concept. The fries are freshly made in front of the customer.
