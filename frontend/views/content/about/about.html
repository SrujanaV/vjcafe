A Little About Our Company


VJ Cafe is as authentic as you can get. VJ Cafe began with the belief that consumers deserved innovation and variation with great quality as well. Our mission is to continue to be recognized as the leading provider of exceptional customer service and superior quality and multiple variety of fries and milkshakes. Since 2015, our standards of quality have never wavered. Everything at VJ Cafe is ruled by taste.


USP - The unique flavors of fries and the most selling, twisted potato. It’s an open kitchen concept. The fries are freshly made in front of the customer.
